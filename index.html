About

Hey! I'm Ed, a freelance software developer based in Oxford.

I have over 4 years experience working in tech, from R&D management for a start-up in London through to developing software for a local consultancy.

In my spare time I teach software development and run a local tech event (Silicon Drinkabout).

I love helping people understand how they can use technology to improve their business.

From creating an SEO-powered website to help your business stand out in your local area, through to showing you out-the-box applications which can save you time and money, I can help you shape and implement your own digital strategy.

What I Offer

Software Development

Website, mobile, desktop... If you need an application, I can help build it for you! All development comes with a free upfront consultation so I can ensure you're solving your problem in the most cost-effective and simplest way possible (why reinvent the wheel if what you need is already available?). Also, I love fixed price work!

Digital Strategy

Tech is an overwhelming world of acronyms, new shiny things, and ever-changing best practices. I stay on top of this so you don't have to, so you can focus on doing what you love most! I offer everything from one-off IT audits through to being your long-term digital partner. Everything starts with a free consultation so I can learn more about your business.

Support

Our project may be finished, but my support isn't! My work comes with a set warranty to fix any potential issues after going live, along with optional support packages to help maintain your new system over the longhaul. If you'd rather maintain it yourself I will always provide full documentation on the project, and can offer teaching services to get you and your team up to speed.

A smile!

I'm not 'just another agency' and you're not 'just another client'! I keep you in the loop, am available via phone or email as and when you need me, and will have a genuine interest in providing the right tech for your business. After all, I'm only as successful as my clients!
